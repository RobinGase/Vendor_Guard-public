# ISO 27001:2022 — Key Control Domains for Vendor Assessment

## A.5 Organizational controls
- A.5.19: Information security in supplier relationships — policies for managing supplier risk
- A.5.20: Addressing security within supplier agreements — contractual requirements
- A.5.21: ICT supply chain security — managing security in the ICT supply chain
- A.5.22: Monitoring, review and change management of supplier services

## A.8 Technological controls
- A.8.2: Privileged access rights — management and review of privileged accounts
- A.8.3: Information access restriction — access based on need-to-know
- A.8.5: Secure authentication — MFA, password policies
- A.8.7: Protection against malware — anti-malware controls
- A.8.8: Management of technical vulnerabilities — patch management process
- A.8.12: Data leakage prevention
- A.8.24: Use of cryptography — encryption at rest and in transit
- A.8.28: Secure coding — SDLC security practices

## Key questions to assess
1. Does the vendor hold a valid ISO 27001 certificate? Is it current and in scope?
2. Is there a documented information security policy?
3. How are privileged accounts managed and reviewed?
4. What is the patch management cadence?
5. Is encryption used for data at rest and in transit?
6. Does the vendor conduct regular penetration testing?
7. Is there a documented incident response procedure?
8. How are subprocessors / sub-suppliers managed?
